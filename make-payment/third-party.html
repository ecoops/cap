Pay through a third party service provider: Payments to the CRA

The CRA does not usually accept payments directly by credit card, PayPal or Interac e-Transfer. PaySimply, a third party service, offers these methods of payments and will send your individual or business payment and remittance details online to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) for you.

Service fees will be charged by the provider.

What you can and can't pay

Payments we accept online through PaySimply
Indivduals
  • Individual income tax amount owing
  • Individual income tax instalment payments
  • Individual COVID-19 benefit repayment (CERB, etc.)
Businesses
  • Canada Child Benefit (CCB) repayment
  • GST/HST credit repayment
  • Canada Dental Benefit repayment
  • One time top-up to Canada Housing Benefit repayment
  • Advanced Canada Workers Benefit repayment
  • Alberta credits repayment
  • Ontario credits repayment
  • Corporation income tax (T2)
  • Corporate income tax instalment payments
  • GST/HST remittances
  • Source deductions (payroll)
  • Excise duties
  • Excise tax
  • Excise tax on insurance premiums
  • Fuel charge
  • Luxury tax
  • Air Travellers Security Charge
  • Softwood lumber products export charge
  • Nova Scotia Workers' Compensation Board payments
  • Underused housing tax
  • COVID-19 business subsidy overpayments (CEWS, etc.)
  • Part XIII withholding tax
  • Trust income tax (T3)
We do not accept the following payments online through PaySimply:
Individuals
  • Individual income tax instalment payments- NO CALC. OPTION
  • Universal Child Care Benefit repayment
  • Climate Action Incentive (CAI) repayment
  • Alberta credits repayment
  • Advanced Canada Workers Benefit repayment
  • TFSA amoung owing
Businesses
  • Information Return (RZ), T5, RRSP, TFSA, etc.
  • Information returns penalty
  • Canada Emergency Rent Subsidy (CERS) repayment
  • Garnishment on behalf of an employee

How to pay the amount you owe

Make a payment to PaySimply, a third party service, which will send your payment and remittance details online to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) for you.

What you need before you pay

You will need the following before you pay:

Individual payments: Option 1 of 2

Your SIN number

Business payments: Option 2 of 2

Your Business Number and program account

Make your payment

To make a payment with PaySimply:

  1. Go to PaySimply's CRA payment page
  2. Review PaySimply's third party service fees and select the type of payment you wish to make
  3. Select your payment method. Options include:
    • Credit card payment
    • PayPal money transfer (Canadian accounts only)
    • Interac e-Transfer

How long it takes to be received and processed

  • Considered as paid: Typically, the same or next (0 to 1) business days
  • Updated in your CRA account: up to X business days after the payment is received. You can confirm if a payment was received.

If your payment still hasn't shown up after 2 business days, resolve a payment issue.

How long the payment takes to be received and processed

  • Considered as paid: the same day it was paid (stamped by the clerk)
  • Updated in your CRA account: up to 2 business days after the payment is received. You can confirm if a payment was received.

If your payment still hasn't shown up after 2 business days, resolve a payment issue.
