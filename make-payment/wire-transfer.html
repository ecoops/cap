Pay at a foreign bank or credit union: Payments to the CRA

Non-residents who do not have a Canadian bank account can make their payments to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) by wire transfer.

Exception: Payments for non-resident GST/HST security deposits cannot be made by wire transfer at this time.

How to pay the amount you owe

Wire transfers are processed in Canada by Fédération des Caisse Desjardins du Québec. They do not charge any fee for forwarding your wire transfer payment to the CRA.

All wire transfers must be made to the CRA in Canadian dollars.

Make sure that your financial institution does not deduct the wire transfer fee from the total payment amount due as this will result in an underpayment.

Your details

Make sure the following details are provided for your payment to be credited to the correct account:

Individual payments: Option 1 of 2

Include the following for an individual payment:

  • non-resident account number or social insurance number
  • full legal name
  • telephone number
  • tax year/year end

Example: 599999976, John Smith, 613-000-0000

Business payments: Option 2 of 2

Include the following for an business payment:

  • non-resident account number or business number
  • business name
  • period end date
  • telephone number

Example: 777777798RP0001, Smith Construction, 2019/03/15, 613-000-0000

Send a copy of your remittance voucher

After sending your wire transfer, send us a copy of your remittance vouncher or GST/HST return/remittance.

We currently only accept your remittance voucher for an international wire transfer by fax to CRA's Revenue Processing Section:

  • Fax number: 204-983-0924
  • Provide the amount paid, the date paid and the confirmation number if available
Generally, your bank should be able to fax this for you.

How long the payment takes to be received and processed

  • Considered as paid: the same or next business day
  • Updated in your CRA account: usually 3 business days after the payment is received. You can confirm if a payment was received.

If your payment still hasn't shown up after 3 business days, resolve a payment issue.
