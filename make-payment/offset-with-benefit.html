How to pay the amount you owe

The payment and debt must both belong to you or your business. We cannot transfer payments between different individuals or your personal account and your business.

How to request an offset

To make an offset, call us.

If you received Form x aka collections

We will automatically make certain offsets if you are in collections. You can call us to request offsets that aren't made automatically, such as for the Canada Child Benefit. Refer to: Offsets

How long it takes to be received and processed

  • Received by CRA: same day it was paid (stamped by the bank employee)
  • Shows up in your CRA account: up to 2 business days after the payment is received. Confirm if a payment was received

If it still hasn't shown up: Resolve a payment issue
